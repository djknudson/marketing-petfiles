Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

PetFiles, operated by Wayward Son Software ("we," "our," or "the app"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

The short version: Your data is stored securely in the cloud so you can access it anywhere. We don't sell your information, we don't track you, and we collect only what's needed to make the app work.

Data We Collect

Data you provide

  • Pet profiles (names, breed, birth date, photos)
  • Vaccination records and dates
  • Vet appointment details
  • Health journal entries
  • Account information (email address and password) when you create an account

Data collected automatically

  • Device type and operating system version (for compatibility)
  • App version (for troubleshooting)
  • Crash reports and error logs (collected anonymously via Sentry, our error monitoring service)

Data not collected

PetFiles does not collect or use your location data. We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA) or any cross-app tracking mechanisms.

Data linked to your identity

Your email address and pet data are linked to your account. Crash reports and diagnostic data are collected anonymously and cannot be connected to you.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the PetFiles app experience
  • To send vaccination and appointment reminders (local notifications scheduled on your device)
  • To store and sync your data so you can access it from any device
  • To diagnose and fix bugs using anonymized crash data

We use your data only for the purposes listed above. We will not repurpose your data for materially different uses without first obtaining your consent.

Data Storage & Security

Your pet data, account information, and uploaded photos are stored on Supabase, a cloud database platform built on PostgreSQL. Supabase encrypts your data in transit (TLS) and at rest using industry-standard encryption. Access to your data is enforced through Row-Level Security, meaning only your authenticated account can read or modify your records.

Authentication credentials are managed by Supabase Auth. Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text.

Data Retention

Your pet data, account information, and uploaded photos are retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (via the in-app deletion feature or by contacting us), all associated data — including pet profiles, health records, and uploaded photos — is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

Anonymized crash reports and diagnostic data collected by Sentry are retained for up to 90 days to allow for bug resolution, then automatically purged.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not share your data with advertising networks or data brokers. We may share anonymized, aggregated analytics (e.g., total number of users) but never individual data.

Third-party services we use:

  • Apple App Store — for app distribution and subscription management
  • Supabase (cloud database) — for storing your account data, pet records, and uploaded photos
  • Sentry (error monitoring) — for collecting anonymized crash reports to improve app stability

PetFiles does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. All third-party service providers listed above are contractually required to protect your data to the same or greater standard as described in this policy.

Tracking

PetFiles does not track you across other companies' apps or websites. We do not use the Apple IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) or any cross-app tracking mechanisms.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored about you
  • Export your pet's health records from the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data via the in-app account deletion feature (Settings → Account → Delete Account)
  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app account management features or contact us at owner@waywardsonsoftware.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

PetFiles is not directed at children under 13. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of PetFiles after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, reach out:

Email: owner@waywardsonsoftware.com

Support page: petfiles.waywardsonsoftware.com/support
