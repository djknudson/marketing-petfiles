Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

PetFiles ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

The short version: Your pet's data stays on your device. We don't sell your information. We collect only what's needed to make the app work.

Data We Collect

Data you provide

  • Pet profiles (names, breed, birth date, photos)
  • Vaccination records and dates
  • Vet appointment details
  • Health journal entries
  • Account information (email) if you create an account for cloud sync

Data collected automatically

  • Device type and operating system version (for compatibility)
  • App version (for troubleshooting)
  • Crash reports (anonymized, via platform crash reporting)

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the PetFiles app experience
  • To send vaccination and appointment reminders (local notifications)
  • To sync your data across devices (if you opt into cloud backup)
  • To diagnose and fix bugs using anonymized crash data

Data Storage & Security

All pet data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable cloud backup, your data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption.

We do not store your data on our servers unless you explicitly opt into cloud sync.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated analytics (e.g., total number of users) but never individual data.

Third-party services we use:

  • Apple App Store — for app distribution and subscription management
  • Cloud infrastructure — for optional encrypted data sync

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored about you
  • Export your pet's health records from the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of cloud sync at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at owner@waywardsonsoftware.com.

Children's Privacy

PetFiles is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of PetFiles after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, reach out:

Email: owner@waywardsonsoftware.com

Support page: petfiles.waywardsonsoftware.com/support
