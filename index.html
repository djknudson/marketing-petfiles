Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is PetFiles really free?

Yes! PetFiles is completely free for one pet. You can track vaccinations, vet appointments, and health records at no cost. Premium unlocks unlimited pets and additional features for $4.99/month.

Is my pet's data secure?

Absolutely. Your data is stored securely on your device and optionally synced via encrypted cloud backup. We never sell your data or share it with third parties.

What types of pets are supported?

PetFiles works for dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, reptiles, and any other pet. If it's your pet, you can track it.

How do reminders work?

PetFiles sends push notifications before upcoming vaccinations and vet appointments. You can customize when and how often you'd like to be reminded.

How do I cancel Premium?

You can cancel anytime through your App Store subscription settings. You'll keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period.

Is PetFiles available in my country?

PetFiles is available worldwide on iOS. Download it from the App Store. Android coming soon.
